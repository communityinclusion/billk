Bill Kiernan Retirement Video Project

On April 4, help us honor Dr. ”Bill” Kiernan’s extraordinary work and commitment over his nearly 50-year career, in achieving greater inclusion for people with disabilities through research, training, education, healthcare, employment, and innovation.

Submit a brief video in advance of April 4, which will be previewed at the event (smartphone clips welcome!) and become part of an important keepsake. This is also a great way to honor Bill if you are unable to make the party.

Your video can be a 10-second “Thanks Bill!” to a slightly longer piece. Feel free to address any or all of the following:

  1. What has Bill meant to the field/done to further the field?
  2. What has Bill meant to you personally? What will you miss about Bill?
  3. Can you share an amusing/interesting story about Bill that we might not know?
